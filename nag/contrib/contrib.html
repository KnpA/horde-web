This is a collection of patches, utilities, and other bits which are useful for Nag. They are not part of the official distribution for various reasons, so you are mostly on your own. If the author has identified themself, you can contact them with questions, and you can always ask on the mailing lists if anyone else is using the modification in question.

NOTE: We cannot verify that these will work on your system, nor are we responsible for any malicious or broken behavior.

Nag 1.0

  • pgsql_nag_to10.sql by Brent J. Nordquist
    • This script will convert an older (pre-1.0) Nag PostgreSQL table to the Nag 1.0 format, by altering it in-place.
