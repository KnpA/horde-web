A Note On Horde

A lot of the examples you'll see in this talk are from the Horde Framework or various Horde applications. While I am obviously biased, this is less a matter of promotion than one of practicality: Horde is what I know and what I have years of real-world experience with, and because of that Horde code is where I can talk about what does - and does not - work.

If you are interested in learning more about Horde, you'll find more information at the following links:

  • Horde website: http://www.horde.org/
  • Horde projects: http://www.horde.org/projects.php
  • Mailing lists: http://www.horde.org/mail/
  • Developer resources: http://dev.horde.org/
  • Presentations: http://www.horde.org/papers/
  • Demos: http://www.horde.org/demo/
