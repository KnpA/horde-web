Looking for dedicated Horde development or support?

Hiring a Horde Project consultant is an effective solution if you need any dedicated Horde resources to meet your project skill requirements or timescale.

The Core Team are the developers that created and are actively developing the Horde Framework and its modules. Its members are available for consultancy for any scale of project. The Core Team can be an excellent choice if you are looking to have a specific functionality or module permanently included in the Horde Project.

If you are interested in the consulting services of the Core Team, send an email to consulting@horde.org with a description of your needs, and someone will get back to you as soon as possible. This email address is for those inquiring about paid services. All other inquiries should be sent to the appropriate mailing list. Messages sent to consulting@horde.org simply asking for help will be forwarded to an appropriate mailing list.
